About Me

I'm Jie Cai (蔡杰)。 I am a PhD candidate in the Ying Wu College of Computing at New Jersey Institute of Technology. I'm supervised by Dr. Yvette Wohn and also work at the Social X Lab . I have been a TA for 5 semesters for 4 courses and independently taught a course. Currently, I am working as a RA with Dr. Yvette Wohn with the NFS-funded project .

My primary area of focus is in human-computer interaction (HCI) with a particular interest in live streaming such as live streaming shopping and content moderation. I applied both survey-based and interview-based research methods to understand users’ motivation for shopping in live streams and how moderators manage harassment in live-chat channels and provide design suggestions to improve user experience in live streaming communities.
